Vivekanand Boys Hostel

Vivekanand Boys Hostel, a distinctive residence within the KIET Group of Institutions,
stands as a purposeful abode catering specifically to second-year and final-year students.
Named after the revered philosopher and spiritual leader Swami Vivekananda, the hostel embodies
his principles of self-discovery and holistic education. At the helm of this nurturing
environment is Warden Amar Bahadur, a dedicated educator committed to fostering
the growth and well-being of the residents.
Catering to the unique needs of second-year and final-year students, Vivekanand Boys Hostel
provides a conducive living space for those transitioning towards the culmination of their
academic journey. Warden Amar Bahadur brings a wealth of experience to his role, ensuring that
the hostel becomes a supportive and enriching home for students in these pivotal years.
In essence, Vivekanand Boys Hostel at the KIET Group of Institutions, guided by Warden
Amar Bahadur, serves as a purposeful residence for second-year and final-year students.
It not only supports their academic endeavors but also fosters a sense of community
and prepares them for the challenges and opportunities that come with the culmination
of their academic and professional journeys.
