Chandragupt Boys Hostel

Chandragupt Boys Hostel, an integral part of the esteemed KIET Group of Institutions,
stands as a dedicated residence specifically tailored for first-year students. Named after
the illustrious Mauryan emperor Chandragupta, the hostel provides a nurturing environment
for the newest members of the academic community. Leading the way in fostering a
supportive living and learning environment is Warden Nagesh Tiwari, a dedicated
educator with a passion for guiding students through their initial year of university life.
Chandragupt Boys Hostel is thoughtfully designed to meet the unique needs of first-year
students, offering a welcoming space for them to transition into the college experience.
Warden Nagesh Tiwari's leadership style combines a sense of discipline with mentorship,
creating an atmosphere that encourages academic excellence and personal growth.
Chandragupt Boys Hostel recognizes the importance of a holistic approach to education
and provides avenues for students to engage in extracurricular activities and cultural
events. This not only enhances their overall college experience but also contributes to
their personal development beyond academics.
In essence, Chandragupt Boys Hostel at the KIET Group of Institutions, under the
leadership of Warden Nagesh Tiwari, serves as a nurturing haven specifically tailored
for first-year students. It lays the foundation for their academic journey, fosters
a sense of community, and prepares them for the exciting challenges that
lie ahead in their university life.
